**Note**: This content policy may be updated or changed at any time without prior notice.

## Use of Machine Learning

Because we do not have staffing to support active moderation by people, content submitted by users is passed through an instance of Tensorflow's [Toxicity machine learning model](https://www.kaggle.com/models/tensorflow/toxicity/) that runs on the user's device. It detects when a user's submission meets the criteria specified below and prevents it from being displayed on the website. Content that it cannot reliably identify as safe or toxic is flagged for human review.

**All content** submitted by users of the website is saved on our servers and may be used to train machine learning models to improve their capabilities.

## Disallowed Content

Content that falls into any of the following categories is prohibited:

1. Hate speech
2. Threats
3. Insults
4. General negativity
5. Obscenities
6. Sexually explicit language
7. Personally identifiable information

## Human Moderation

The community at large may report content that they feel does not abide by the content policy. The content will be unpublished immediately until such time that it can be reviewed by a human. Content found to violate the policy will remain unpublished and content found to be compliant will be republished.

Project administrators are granted the right to publish or unpublish user submissions at any time and for any reason that they are not required to disclose. Their decisions are final and are not open to rebuttals, arguments, appeals, or second looks.

### False Reports

Human resources available for moderation are extremely limited. Do not take away from what little time there is by submitting false reports.
