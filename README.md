## Features

- Share your favorite things and watch it show up on the globe right where you're standing.
- See things that others have shared around the world, or take a minute to admire the view from outer space.

## Inspiration

In the movie, [The Space Between Us](https://www.imdb.com/title/tt3922818/), the main character, who spent his whole life on Mars before coming to Earth, asks people he meets: "What is your favorite thing about earth?"

This is a question that I think people don't stop to consider often enough and by sharing this website, I hope that it might change that.

By the way, don't go watch that film unless you really like super cheesy romance movies.
